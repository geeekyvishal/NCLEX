# Business Plan - NCLEX AI Flashcard & Practice App

*Draft v1 - July 2026*

---

## 1. One-Line Pitch
The study app built for NCLEX: AI flashcards for what you must memorize, plus Next-Gen (NGN) clinical-judgment practice for what actually gets tested.

---

## 2. Problem
About 300,000+ students sit the NCLEX each year.
They are anxious, high-stakes, and English-speaking.
Flashcard tools (Anki, Quizlet) require manual card creation and are intimidating.
AI flashcard apps (StudyFetch, Turbo AI) charge $19-20/mo and are widely called overpriced.
**Critical gap:** the Next-Gen NCLEX shifted heavily to clinical-judgment formats (matrix, case studies, trend analysis) that plain flashcards cannot train.
Existing flashcard apps ignore this.

---

## 3. Solution
A single app that covers both study layers NCLEX demands.

1. **Memorization layer** - AI generates accurate, non-bloated flashcards from any PDF or notes (pharmacology, lab values, safety thresholds).
2. **Clinical-judgment layer** - AI generates NGN-style practice questions (matrix, case studies) that no pure-flashcard app offers.
3. **Retention engine** - real FSRS spaced repetition plus one-click Anki export.

**Positioning:** *"Flashcards for what you must memorize + Next-Gen practice for what actually gets tested - built for NCLEX."*

---

## 4. Target Market
**Beachhead:** US/Canada NCLEX-RN & NCLEX-PN candidates (~300k/yr).
**Why nursing, not USMLE:** 6x bigger pool, weaker incumbent lock-in (no AnKing equivalent), softer competition, content better suited to flashcards, and a clear NGN gap.
**Expansion later:** USMLE (up-market), then international English exams.
India comes later via a B2B or one-time-pay model, not individual subscriptions.

---

## 5. Competitive Landscape

| Competitor | Price | Weakness we exploit |
|-----------|-------|--------------------|
| Anki | Free | Manual, ugly, no AI, no NGN |
| Quizlet | ~$7.99/mo | AI paywalled, added friction |
| Knowt | Free | Weak spaced repetition, no NGN |
| StudyFetch / Turbo | $19-20/mo | Overpriced, general (not NCLEX-specific), no NGN focus |
| UWorld | Premium | Great QBank but expensive; flashcards locked to full-course users |

**Our wedge:** NCLEX-specific, accurate (not bloated), covers NGN, and roughly 1/4 the price of StudyFetch.

---

## 6. Monetization
**Free tier:** limited decks plus the no-signup demo (acquisition engine).
**Paid:** $4.99-6.99/mo or one-time credit packs.
Headline: *"1/4 the price of StudyFetch."*
**Seasonal push:** demand spikes around NCLEX exam windows and nursing-school finals.
**Later:** B2B deals with nursing programs and cohorts, plus affiliate on prep materials.

---

## 7. Go-To-Market (first 100 to first 1,000)
**First 100 (see Action Plan):** r/NCLEX launch post, TikTok/Reels screen demos, FB nursing groups, nano-influencers, cohort chats.
Spend is $0-100.
**Scale to 1,000:** repeatable short-form video format, referral loop (share deck for credits), studygram nano-influencer roster, and SEO on "NCLEX flashcards / NGN practice."
Distribution is the moat, not the app.
Whoever markets best in the nursing community wins.

---

## 8. Key Metrics
Activation: percent who generate a deck (target above 60% of visitors via the no-signup demo).
Retention: percent returning within 7 days (early product-market-fit signal).
Free-to-paid conversion: target 3-5%.
Referral coefficient: shares per active user.

---

## 9. Risks & Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|
| AI card accuracy (dangerous for medical content) | Human-flag + AI-fix loop; confidence indicators; curated NCLEX templates |
| NGN questions hard to generate well | Start narrow (matrix + case studies), expand as quality proves out |
| Price war with cheap apps | Pair price with NCLEX-specificity + NGN, not price alone |
| Incumbents add NGN | Move fast, own the nursing community before they notice |

---

## 10. 90-Day Milestones
**Day 14:** 100 users, feedback loop running.
**Day 30:** 500 users, first paying customers, one repeatable video format.
**Day 60:** 1,000+ users, referral loop live, 3-5% conversion validated.
**Day 90:** decide scale channel and whether to raise or reinvest; scope USMLE expansion.
