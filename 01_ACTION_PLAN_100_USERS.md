# Action Plan - Hit First 100 Users ASAP

**Product:** AI NCLEX flashcard + Next-Gen practice app
**Target:** US/Canada nursing students preparing for NCLEX-RN/PN
**Goal:** 100 real users in 14 days, ~$0-100 spend
**Core principle:** Do things that don't scale.
First 100 users are hand-to-hand, not marketing.

---

## The One Rule That Makes Everything Work

**No-signup free demo.**
Anyone can paste a PDF, get flashcards, without creating an account.
Ask for email ONLY when they want to save or export a deck.

> If you gate value behind signup, conversion collapses.
> Let them feel the magic first.

---

## The 5 Channels (ranked by speed-to-first-users)

### 1. Reddit - r/NCLEX + r/StudentNurse (best single source)
Thousands of anxious students ask for study help daily.
**Launch post:** *"I built a free NCLEX flashcard generator - giving it to the first 50 people who want to test it. Comment your weakest topic."*
**Daily:** Reply genuinely to "drowning in pharm, help" posts, then offer to run their PDF for free.
Do not spam.
Be a real member first.

### 2. TikTok / Instagram Reels - #nursingschool #nclex
Screen-record a demo: *"POV: NCLEX in 2 weeks and you haven't touched pharm."*
Upload a nursing PDF, cards appear, "free, link in bio."
Post 1-2 per day.
You don't need to go viral - one 500-view video sends about 20 people.

### 3. Facebook Groups (underrated - nurses live here)
"NCLEX Study Group," "Nursing Students 2026" - many have 50k-200k members.
Join 10, be helpful for a few days, then share.
Ask admins first and offer them free access.

### 4. Nano-influencers (fastest paid-ish route)
DM 10 nursing studygrammers with 5k-30k followers.
Offer free lifetime access plus $20-50 for one honest demo video.
One post can send 50-100 students overnight.

### 5. Direct cohort outreach
Nursing programs run in tight cohorts with GroupMe/WhatsApp chats.
One student sharing in their class chat can reach 30 classmates.

## Viral Loops to Bake In From Day 1
"Share this deck with a classmate" gives both people free premium credits.
Shared decks are public with your watermark, so every study group advertises you.
After a good session, prompt: *"NCLEX in X days? Share your streak."*

---

## Traps to Avoid
Do not build in silence for 3 months then "launch."
Post BEFORE the app is polished.
Do not require signup before value.
Do not try to reach millions - you need a few hundred right people.
Do not ignore feedback - your first 100 users ARE your product research.

---

## Definition of Done (Week 2)
- [ ] 100+ signups
- [ ] 20+ users who came back a second time (retention signal)
- [ ] 5+ pieces of feedback on what topics or features they want
- [ ] 1 TikTok that broke 1,000 views (repeatable format found)
